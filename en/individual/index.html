Individual sessions

We provide a professional, confidential, and comfortable environment for a partners in a couple to understand each other. Duration of one session 1h. Once we agree to work together, the minimum number of counseling sessions is at least 6, others depend on the difficulty and depth of the couple’s situation. Depending on the situation either one or both therapists are present.

What can you expect?

Support when making decisions. Learning emotional autonomy - being able to feel fine even when your partner is undergoing tough times. Understanding your input in a sustainable relationship. Understanding your needs, vulnerabilities. Growing resilience to manipulations. Support when undergoing emotional crisis: unfaithfulness, divorce, loss.

Rate

Hourly rate – from € 40 to € 60.
